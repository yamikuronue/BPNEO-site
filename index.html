What is Body Positivity?

It's simple: body positivity believes that all bodies are worthy. You're a person, and you have a body, and that body is magnificent, no matter what it looks like: ALL bodies are good bodies. If you don't love your body yet, that's fine, just don't hate on it in here :) We don't tolerate talk about changing your body or anyone else's body -- no weight loss talk, no surgery talk, no racism, no transphobia. Just love.

Who are we?

We're fat people, thin people, young people, old people, male, female, and everything in between. We're united by two things: our commitment to radical body love, and our geographic location.
